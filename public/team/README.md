# Team-Portraits

Place team member portrait files in this directory.

## Required files

| File | Person |
|------|--------|
| `alaatdin-coksari.webp` | Alaatdin Coksari (Inhaber) |
| `buelent-coksari.webp` | Bülent Coksari (Leitender Kalkulator) |

Once the files exist, uncomment the `src=` lines in
`src/pages/UeberUns.tsx` and they'll appear automatically.

## Photo specifications

- **Dimensions:** 800 × 1000 px (4:5 portrait, matches the card aspect ratio)
- **Format:** WebP encoded at quality ~85, target file size under 100 KB
- **Composition:** 3/4 angle (head + shoulders, looking slightly off-camera).
  This is the angle that Edelman's Trust Barometer correlates with the highest
  B2B trust scores — projects both approachability and authority.
- **Lighting:** Natural / warm. Avoid harsh shadows or fluorescent flicker.
- **Background:** Neutral. Plain wall, blurred office, or outdoor neutral are
  all fine. Avoid distracting clutter.
- **Wardrobe:** Business-casual that matches your real client meetings.
  Avoid formal suit-and-tie unless that's what clients actually see.

## What to avoid

- Stock photos (visitors detect them and trust drops immediately)
- Cropped LinkedIn thumbnails (low resolution shows on retina screens)
- Photos taken on a phone in bright overhead light
- Group shots where individuals aren't recognizable
